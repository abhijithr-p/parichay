Event Rules

Rules to Follow

  • Each participant can take part in only one solo event and one team event.
  • Participants must carry their college ID card at all times.
  • Any kind of misconduct or cheating will result in disqualification.
  • Teams/participants failing to report on time will be disqualified automatically.
  • Decisions made by the judges will be final and binding.
  • Participants must maintain discipline and follow campus rules.
  • Damage to college property will lead to penalty and disciplinary action.
  • The organizing team reserves the right to change event timings or rules if needed.

UNTANGLE THE TANGLE

  • Each team must consist of 2 members(from the same or different departments).
  • Open to all TJIT students interested in coding and problem-solving.
  • Usage of phones, laptops, books, or the internet is strictly prohibited during the event.
  • Any kind of malpractice will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Any kind of malpractice leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Decisions made by the judges are final and binding.
  • Tiebreakers (if needed) will be decided by an additional coding challenge.
  • Teams must report 10 minutes before the competition begins.
  • Late entries will not be entertained once the round starts.
  • Code must be written in the provided coding environment.
  • Time limits for each round must be strictly adhered to.

QUIZATHON

  • 2 members per team (from the same or different departments).
  • Open to all TJIT students interested in technology
  • No external help allowed during competition.
  • Use of electronic devices, books, or internet during the quiz is strictly prohibited.
  • Any kind of malpractice leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Decisions made by the quiz master/judges are final and binding.
  • Tiebreakers (if needed) will be decided by an extra round of questions.
  • Teams must report 10 minutes before the quiz begins.
  • Late entries will not be entertained once the round starts.
  • For written rounds, answers must be marked clearly.
  • For buzzer or oral rounds, answers must be given within the time limit.

COOK LIKE A LOCAL

  • 2 members per team
  • No use of fire or electrical appliances (like stove, microwave, or induction).
  • Only raw ingredients or ready-to-eat items are allowed.
  • Participants must bring their own ingredients and utensils.
  • Cleanliness and hygiene must be maintained throughout.
  • Presentation and creativity are important; decorate your dish attractively.
  • Participants must explain the name, ingredients, and preparation method of their dish to the judges.
  • Use of healthy and eco-friendly ingredients is encouraged.
  • Plagiarism (copying others) is not allowed.
  • Judges decision will be final and binding.

OBSTACRUSH

  • 4 members per team; no changes after registration.
  • Teams must stay together, no splitting.
  • No tampering with or removing other team's clues.
  • No phones, internet or external help unless allowed.
  • Fair play is mandatory — cheating = disqualification.
  • No unsafe or illegal actions.
  • Inform organizers immediately if anyone is injured.
  • Organizer's decision is final.

PHOTOGRAPHY

  • Open to all registered participants.
  • Theme will be given on-spot.
  • Only 1 photo per participant.
  • Participants must bring their own cameras or smartphones.
  • Photo must be clicked during the event.
  • Photo must be submitted within an hour after the event starts.
  • Only basic editing allowed (crop, brightness, color correction).
  • No AI-generated or stock images.
  • Do not disturb others or violate privacy.
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding.
  • No unsafe or prohibited activities.

FREEFIRE

  • Each team consists of 4 players only (no substitutes).
  • Only Android and iOS devices are allowed.
  • Emulators and iPads are strictly prohibited.
  • Any use of game-modifying tools will lead to disqualification.
  • In-game voice chat is mandatory for proper team coordination.
  • Use of unfair means such as aimbot, trigger bot, or ESP will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Bug exploitation or any act that hinders fair play will lead to disqualification.
  • Players must join 15 minutes before the match starts.
  • In case of a tie, the number of Booyahs (wins) will decide the champion.
