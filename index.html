About Parichay

Parichay is a techfest organized by the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) department of T John Institute of Technology. The event aims to ignite student's passion for technology by providing a platform to explore, innovate, and compete in various technical domains. Through engaging competitions, workshops, and interactive sessions, Parichay fosters a culture of learning, creativity, and problem-solving, encouraging students to enhance their technical knowledge and skills.

Events

UNTANGLE THE TANGLE

Two member coding event where teams arrange jumbled code snippets in the correct sequence and produce accurate outputs within a time limit, testing logic, problem solving, and technical skills.

COOK LIKE A LOCAL

Teams of two prepared traditional hometown dishes without fire, showcasing innovation, nutrition, and cultural heritage through creative, healthy recipes that reflected their regional flavors.

QUIZATHON

The Technical Quiz featured two rounds testing participants technical knowledge and problem solving skills, from core concepts to advanced challenges, to crown the ultimate tech champions.

OBSTACRUSH

Teams of four will solve technical and non technical clues, racing to complete all challenges and find the “treasure” to win.

PHOTOGRAPHY

Participants will showcase creativity through photographs that tell powerful stories beyond words.

FREEFIRE

Teams of four will battle in an intense Free Fire showdown, testing teamwork, strategy, and survival to claim victory.
