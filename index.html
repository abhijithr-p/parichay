About Parichay

Parichay is a techfest organized by the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) department of T John Institute of Technology. The event aims to ignite students’ passion for technology by providing a platform to explore, innovate, and compete in various technical domains.

Events

UNTANGLE THE TANGLE

Arrange jumbled code snippets correctly within time to test logic and skill.

COOK LIKE A LOCAL

Teams prepare innovative, fireless dishes that reflect their regional flavors.

QUIZATHON

Technical quiz testing participants’ knowledge and problem-solving abilities.

OBSTACRUSH

Teams solve clues and race to complete challenges in a thrilling treasure hunt.

PHOTOGRAPHY

Capture creativity through photos that tell stories beyond words.

FREEFIRE

Teams of four battle it out in an intense Free Fire showdown to claim victory.
